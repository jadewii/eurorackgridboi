Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy digital packs to get physical stickers?

Yes! You must unlock a module digitally before you can order its physical sticker. However, the $80 Ultimate pack includes ALL 25 physical stickers immediately, bypassing the unlock requirement.

What are the different rarities?

Modules come in four rarities: Common (gray), Rare (blue), Epic/Holographic (purple with shimmer effect), and Legendary (gold with animated effects). Rarer modules are harder to find but more valuable for trading!

Can I trade my modules?

Yes! The marketplace allows you to trade modules with other collectors. You can only trade module-for-module (no selling for rupees), keeping the economy balanced and fun.

What happens to extra rupees?

Rupees never expire! Save them for future pack releases, special events, or use them to keep opening packs to complete your collection.

Are the physical stickers good quality?

Absolutely! All physical stickers are high-quality vinyl, perfect for decorating your eurorack setup, laptop, or anywhere else. They're weatherproof and designed to last.
